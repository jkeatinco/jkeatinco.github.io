Life on Mars... 👽 🚀 🌌

is a collection of hundreds of previously black and white raw images from the Mars Curiosity Rover ran through DeOldify back in 2020. Fast forward 4 AI years, I have now upsacled them all using models in ComfyUI! These images are not meant to be an accurate representation of the true color on the surface of Mars but will hopefully offer you an insightful new perspective of the planet.
